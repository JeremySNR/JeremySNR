Calibration target

The 80% prediction interval should cover ~80% of holdout points (target band 75-85%). A value far from 80% indicates miscalibration that the conformal correction did not fully absorb.

Methodology

  1. LightGBM regressor on log(price) with native categorical handling.
  2. Forward-chaining time split (oldest 70% train, middle 15% calibration, newest 15% test) to avoid leaking the 2020-22 COVID regime shift.
  3. Conformalized Quantile Regression (CQR) on the calibration set for the 80% interval.
  4. Empirical-Bayes hierarchical shrinkage on residuals by (model_family x era_segment).
  5. Duan-smearing bias correction on the log -> dollar back-transform.
